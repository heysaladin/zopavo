## A. REVISION POLICY

### A1. What counts as a revision round

A revision round is **one consolidated set of feedback** submitted at the same time. All comments for a given round should be collected and sent together. Feedback sent in multiple messages over multiple days counts as the same round, provided they relate to the same deliverable version.

### A2. Revision rounds included

| Deliverable type | Revision rounds included |
|---|---|
| Logo concept / direction | 2 rounds |
| Brand identity refinement | 2 rounds |
| Brand guidelines document | 1 round |
| Website design per page | 2 rounds |
| Development / build | 1 round |
| Motion / animation | 2 rounds |
| Print collateral | 1 round |
| Other (as specified in SOW) | As stated |

### A3. What constitutes a revision vs. a new brief

**Revisions (included):** Changes to elements within the agreed direction — e.g. adjust colour, tweak typography, resize layout.

**New brief / out of scope (billed separately):**
- Changing the concept direction after approval
- Adding new pages, screens, or sections not in the original SOW
- Requesting a style that was not discussed in the brief
- Recreating something from scratch after approval

### A4. Additional revision rounds

If all included rounds are used and further revisions are needed:

| Type | Rate |
|---|---|
| Minor revisions (< 1h work) | IDR [X] per round |
| Standard additional round | IDR [X] per round |
| Hourly rate (for complex rework) | IDR [X]/hour |

Additional rounds must be agreed in writing before work begins.

Full month fee due on the **1st of each month**, before work for that month begins. No refunds for unused capacity within the month.

### B4. Accepted payment methods

- Bank transfer (BCA / Mandiri / BNI — Indonesia)
- [Wise / PayPal — for international clients, [X]% international fee applies]
- Other methods by prior agreement

### B5. Late payment

- Payment is due within **[7] business days** of invoice date
- After [14] days: [1.5]% per month late fee applies to outstanding balance

All deliverables, files, and source materials remain the intellectual property of Hyperfantasy Studio until the final payment is received in full. Delivery of files does not constitute transfer of ownership prior to payment.
