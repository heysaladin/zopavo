## Agenda

### 1. Opening (2 min)
- Warm welcome, brief intro of who's on the call
- "Thanks for your time — today's goal is just to understand your project better and see if we're a good match. No pressure at all."

### 2. Client intro (5 min)
- "Tell us a bit about your brand / company — what you do and who you serve."
- "What's the origin story — how did you get here?"

### 3. Project deep-dive (15 min)

**Key questions to ask:**
- [ ] "What's the main problem this project needs to solve?"
- [ ] "What does success look like 6 months after launch?"
- [ ] "Are there brands or references you love — and why?"
- [ ] "What's your biggest concern about this project?"
- [ ] "Have you worked with a studio before? What worked / didn't work?"

**Notes:**
> [Live notes during call]

### 4. Timeline & budget (5 min)
- [ ] "Is there a launch date or event driving the timeline?"
- [ ] "Have you set a budget for this? Happy to be direct."
- [ ] "Do you need to involve anyone else in the decision?"

### 5. Our approach (5 min)
- Brief overview of how Hyperfantasy works (phase overview, collaboration style)
- "We typically work in [X] phases — from brief to handover. Here's roughly how that looks for a project like yours…"
- Share 1 relevant case study or reference if appropriate

### 6. Next steps (3 min)
- [ ] "Based on what you've shared, here's what I think makes sense as a next step…"
- Proposal timeline: "We'll follow up with a proposal within [3 business days]"
- Or: "I'd like to discuss internally and get back to you by [DATE]"
- Confirm best contact / email for proposal delivery
