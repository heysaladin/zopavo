**Hyperfantasy Studio | Marketing Template**
**Owner:** Marketing Bot

---

## How to Use

Replace all `[BRACKETS]` with specific details before sending. Choose the template that matches the lead source. Always personalise the first line.

---

## Template 1 — Cold Email (Portfolio-led)

**Subject:** Your brand + [SPECIFIC OBSERVATION]

---

Hi [FIRST NAME],

[Personalised opener — one sentence about something specific to their brand/work.]

I'm [YOUR NAME] from Hyperfantasy Studio — we design brands and digital experiences for [industry type] companies across Southeast Asia and the Middle East.

We recently worked with [RELEVANT REFERENCE CLIENT] on [brief outcome], and I noticed [their/your company] is at a similar stage.

Would it make sense to have a quick 20-minute call to see if we could help?

Here's a recent case study that might be relevant: [LINK]

[YOUR NAME]
Hyperfantasy Studio
[CONTACT / CALENDLY LINK]

## Do's and Don'ts

| Do | Don't |
|---|---|
| Personalise the opening line | Start with "I hope this email finds you well" |
| Reference specific work or pain | Send generic mass emails |
| Include one clear CTA | Include multiple links or asks |
| Follow up twice max | Chase more than 3 times |
| Keep it under 150 words | Write paragraphs of backstory |
